Must Visit @ CHHATTISGARH

Chhattisgarh is often referred to as the "Bread Basket of Central India" because of its rich agricultural output. Its capital city is RAIPUR.

(1)Chitrakote Falls: Often referred to as the "Niagara Falls of India," Chitrakote is the widest waterfall in the country, located on the Indravati River.

(2)Bastar: Known for its tribal culture, Bastar offers unique experiences like the Bastar Dussehra festival and traditional handicrafts.

(3)Tirathgarh Waterfall: A picturesque waterfall located in the Kanger Valley National Park, surrounded by lush greenery.

(4)Kanger Valley National Park: A biodiversity hotspot with dense forests, limestone caves, and wildlife, including the rare Bastar hill myna.

(5)Sirpur: An archaeological site with ancient temples, monasteries, and Buddhist ruins, reflecting the rich history of the region.

(6)Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary: Home to a variety of flora and fauna, including leopards, elephants, and various bird species.

(7)Danteshwari Temple, Dantewada: A sacred temple dedicated to Goddess Danteshwari, revered by the local people and an important pilgrimage site.

(8)Mahant Ghasidas Memorial Museum: Located in Raipur, the museum showcases the history, art, and culture of Chhattisgarh.

(9)Bambleshwari Temple, Dongargarh: A hilltop temple dedicated to Goddess Bambleshwari, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

(10)Rajpuri Waterfall: A serene waterfall located near Kanger Valley National Park, offering a tranquil natural setting.
