Must Visit @ HARAYANA

Haryana is often referred to as the "Green Land of India" because of its agricultural productivity and the lush greenery in certain regions. Its capital city is CHANDIGARH.

(1)Sultanpur National Park: A bird sanctuary near Gurugram, known for migratory birds, bird watching, and nature trails.

(2)Surajkund: Famous for the annual Surajkund International Crafts Mela, showcasing traditional crafts and cultural performances.

(3)Rock Garden, Chandigarh: An artistic garden made entirely from industrial and home waste, created by Nek Chand.

(4)Pinjore Gardens: Also known as Yadavindra Gardens, it is an Mughal garden with terraced lawns, fountains, and historic structures.

(5)Bhishma Kund: A historical reservoir associated with the Mahabharata, named after Bhishma Pitamah.

(6)Baba Thakur Das Ropeway, Yamunanagar: An adventurous cable car ride offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

(7)Damdama Lake, Sohna: A large natural reservoir surrounded by the Aravalli Hills, offering boating and other recreational activities.

(8)Morni Hills: A hill station in the Panchkula district, known for its scenic beauty, trekking trails, and the Tikkar Taal Lake.

(9)Kalesar National Park: A wildlife sanctuary with diverse flora and fauna, located in the Yamunanagar district.

(10)Devi Bhawan Mandir, Panipat: A temple dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi, known for its religious significance.
