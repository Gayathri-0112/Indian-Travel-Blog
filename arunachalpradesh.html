Must Visit @ ARUNACHAL PRADESH

Arunachal Pradesh is often referred to as the "Land of the Dawn-Lit Mountains." Its capital city is ITANAGAR.

(1)Tawang Monastery: A renowned Buddhist monastery, one of the largest in India, located in the scenic town of Tawang.

(2)Ziro Valley: Known for its picturesque landscapes and the unique Apatani tribal culture, designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

(3)Bomdila Monastery: A Buddhist monastery in Bomdila offering serene surroundings and panoramic views of the Himalayas.

(4)Sela Pass: A high-altitude mountain pass with breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, lakes, and vibrant rhododendron forests.

(5)Namdapha National Park: One of the largest national parks in India, home to diverse flora and fauna, including the elusive snow leopard.

(6)Dirang Valley: Surrounded by apple orchards and beautiful landscapes, featuring hot water springs and the Sangti Valley.

(7)Pangchenpa: A small village known for its vibrant festivals and traditional customs, offering a glimpse into the local way of life.

(8)Bhalukpong: A nature lover's paradise with lush greenery, the Kameng River, and opportunities for river rafting and trekking.

(9)Itanagar: The capital city, featuring the Ita Fort, Ganga Lake, and the State Museum showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Arunachal Pradesh.

(10)Nuranang Falls (Jang Falls): A stunning waterfall near Tawang, surrounded by pristine landscapes and accessible through a picturesque drive.
