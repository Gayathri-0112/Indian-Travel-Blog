Must Visit @ HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himachal Pradesh is often referred to as the "Land of Gods" due to its scenic landscapes, numerous temples, and spiritual significance. Its capital city is SHIMLA.

(1)Shimla: The capital city, known for its colonial architecture, the Ridge, Mall Road, and the historic Christ Church.

(2)Manali: A popular hill station with breathtaking landscapes, Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and adventure activities like paragliding.

(3)Chamba: Known for its ancient temples, the Chamba Palace, and the scenic Chamera Lake.

(4)Manikaran: Famous for its hot springs, the Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara, and the Harinder Mountain.

(5)Spiti Valley: A high-altitude desert valley with monasteries like Key Monastery, picturesque landscapes, and adventure trails.

(6)Kasol: A serene village on the banks of the Parvati River, popular among backpackers and nature lovers.

(7)Dalhousie: A hill station with colonial architecture, the Khajjiar meadow, and the Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary.

(8)Kufri: A small hill station near Shimla, known for its scenic beauty, Mahasu Peak, and the Himalayan Nature Park.

(9)Bir Billing: A renowned paragliding destination, also known for its Tibetan monasteries and the Deer Park Institute.

(10)Kasauli: A charming hill station with colonial architecture, the Christ Church, and the Monkey Point.
