Must Visit @ KERALA

Kerala is often referred to as the "God's Own Country" due to its stunning natural beauty, diverse landscapes, and tranquil backwaters. Its capital city is THIRUVANANTHAPURAM.

(1)Vembanad Lake: The longest lake in India, offering scenic backwater cruises and houseboat experiences.

(2)Athirappilly Waterfalls: A majestic waterfall in Thrissur district, surrounded by lush greenery and known for its scenic beauty.

(3)Mattancherry Palace (Dutch Palace): A historic palace in Kochi known for its stunning mural paintings depicting Hindu epics.

(4)Varkala Cliff: A picturesque cliff overlooking the Arabian Sea in Varkala, offering stunning sunset views.

(5)Ponmudi: A hill station near Thiruvananthapuram, known for its lush green landscapes and pleasant climate.

(6)Chembra Peak: The highest peak in Wayanad, offering trekking opportunities and panoramic views.

(7)Kanakakkunnu Palace: A heritage palace in Thiruvananthapuram, hosting cultural events and exhibitions.

(8)Parambikulam Tiger Reserve: A wildlife sanctuary known for its biodiversity, including the Bengal tiger.

(9)Kumbalangi Integrated Tourism Village: A model fishing village near Kochi, providing an authentic rural experience.

(10)Munroe Island: A serene island in the backwaters of Kollam, known for its canoe tours and homestay experiences.
