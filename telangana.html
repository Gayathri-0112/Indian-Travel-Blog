Must Visit @ TELANGANA

Telangana is often called the "Gateway to the South" because of its geographical location as a central southern state in India. Its capital city is AMARAVATI.

(1)Hyderabad: The capital city known for the Charminar, Golkonda Fort, Hussain Sagar Lake, and the vibrant Laad Bazaar.

(2)Warangal: Famous for the Thousand Pillar Temple, Warangal Fort, and the Kakatiya Musical Garden.

(3)Nagarjuna Sagar: Home to the Nagarjuna Sagar Dam, one of the largest dams in the world, and the Buddhist archaeological site of Nagarjunakonda.

(4)Bhadrachalam: A pilgrimage town on the banks of the Godavari River, known for the Bhadrachalam Temple dedicated to Lord Rama.

(5)Mahbubnagar: Home to the historic Gadwal Fort, Pillalamarri Banyan Tree, and the Mallela Theertham Waterfall.

(6)Nalgonda: Offers attractions like Bhongir Fort, Nagarjuna Sagar-Srisailam Tiger Reserve, and the Panagal Reservoir.

(7)Yadagirigutta: A pilgrimage site known for the Yadadri Lakshmi Narasimha Temple.

(8)Chilkur Balaji Temple: A popular temple known as the "Visa Balaji Temple," believed to fulfill wishes related to visas.

(9)Papikondalu: A scenic location on the Godavari River, known for its picturesque landscapes and boat cruises.

(10)Sangareddy: Known for the heritage site of Sangareddy Fort, Manjeera Wildlife Sanctuary, and the Kaulas Fort.
