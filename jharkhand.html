Must Visit @ JHARKHAND

Jharkhand is often referred to as the "Land of Forests" due to its rich and diverse forest cover. Its capital city is RANCHI.

(1)Hundru Falls: A picturesque waterfall near Ranchi, known for its scenic beauty and natural surroundings.

(2)Netarhat: Referred to as the "Queen of Chotanagpur," Netarhat is a hill station with beautiful landscapes and pleasant weather.

(3)Dassam Falls: A stunning waterfall on the Subarnarekha River, offering a peaceful natural setting.

(4)Jagannath Temple, Ranchi: A modern architectural marvel, this temple is a prominent religious site in the capital city.

(5)Betla National Park: One of the first national parks in India, Betla is known for its wildlife, including tigers, elephants, and deer.

(6)Jonha Falls: Also known as Gautamdhara, this waterfall is surrounded by dense forests and is considered sacred.

(7)Parasnath Hill: A significant Jain pilgrimage site with 23 temples atop the hill, dedicated to different Jain Tirthankaras.

(8)Sun Temple, Maruadih: A modern Sun Temple with unique architecture, attracting visitors for its spiritual ambiance.

(9)Hathi Pahar: A small hill with a large rock resembling an elephant, offering panoramic views of Ranchi city.

(10)Tagore Hill: Named after Rabindranath Tagore, this hill offers panoramic views of Ranchi and its surroundings.
