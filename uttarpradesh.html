Must Visit @ UTTAR PRADESH

Uttar Pradesh is often referred to as the "Heartland of India" due to its central location in the country. Its capital city is LUCKNOW.

(1)Agra: Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Fatehpur Sikri, all UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

(2)Varanasi: The spiritual and cultural hub, known for the ghats along the Ganges, Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and Sarnath.

(3)Lucknow: The capital city known for Bara Imambara, Chota Imambara, Rumi Darwaza, and the vibrant street food.

(4)Ayodhya: A sacred city known for Ram Janmabhoomi, Hanuman Garhi, and other temples associated with Lord Rama.

(5)Mathura-Vrindavan: Birthplace of Lord Krishna, with attractions like Krishna Janmabhoomi, Banke Bihari Temple, and ISKCON Temple.

(6)Allahabad (Prayagraj): Famous for the Triveni Sangam, Kumbh Mela, Allahabad Fort, and Anand Bhavan.

(7)Noida and Greater Noida: Urban centers with shopping malls, amusement parks, and the Buddh International Circuit.

(8)Sarnath: A Buddhist pilgrimage site near Varanasi, known for the Dhamek Stupa and the Ashoka Pillar.

(9)Fatehpur: Known for the architectural marvel of Fatehpur Sikri, including Buland Darwaza and Jama Masjid.

(10)Kushinagar: A significant Buddhist pilgrimage site, where Lord Buddha is believed to have attained Mahaparinirvana.
