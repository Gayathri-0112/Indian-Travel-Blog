Must Visit @ GOA

Goa is often referred to as the "Pearl of the Orient" due to its scenic beauty, pristine beaches, and vibrant culture. Its capital city is PANAJI.

(1)Beaches: Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, Anjuna Beach: Famous for their golden sands, water sports, and vibrant beach shacks.

(2)Fort Aguada: A well-preserved Portuguese fort with panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, known for its historic lighthouse.

(3)Basilica of Bom Jesus: A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Old Goa, housing the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier.

(4)Dudhsagar Waterfalls: A majestic four-tiered waterfall set amidst lush greenery, accessible by trekking or jeep safari.

(5)Anjuna Flea Market: A vibrant market where you can shop for clothing, jewelry, and souvenirs, reflecting Goa's bohemian culture.

(6)Dona Paula: A viewpoint with stunning views of the Arabian Sea, named after a historical figure, and known for its romantic aura.

(7)Se Cathedral: One of the largest churches in Asia, located in Old Goa, known for its Portuguese-Gothic architecture.

(8)Chapora Fort: Known for its iconic location overlooking Vagator Beach, featured in the Bollywood movie "Dil Chahta Hai."

(9)Naval Aviation Museum: Located in Vasco da Gama, it showcases the evolution of Indian Naval Aviation with aircraft and exhibits.

(10)Tito's Lane and Club Cubana: Famous nightlife spots in North Goa, offering lively clubs, bars, and a vibrant party scene.
