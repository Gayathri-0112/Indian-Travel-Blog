Must Visit @ UTTARAKHAND

Uttarakhand is often referred to as the "Devbhumi" or the "Land of Gods" due to its numerous Hindu temples and pilgrimage sites. Its capital city is DEHRADUN.

(1)Rishikesh: Known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," it offers spiritual experiences, river rafting, and the iconic Laxman Jhula.

(2)Haridwar: A sacred city on the banks of the Ganges, known for Ganga Aarti, Har Ki Pauri, and spiritual significance.

(3)Dehradun: The capital city known for Robber's Cave, Forest Research Institute, and the iconic Clock Tower.

(4)Mussoorie: A popular hill station with attractions like Kempty Falls, Camel's Back Road, and Gun Hill.

(5)Nainital: Famous for Naini Lake, Snow View Point, Naina Devi Temple, and the Mall Road.

(6)Corbett National Park: India's oldest national park, known for wildlife safaris and the chance to spot Bengal tigers.

(7)Badrinath: A major pilgrimage site with the Badrinath Temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

(8)Kedarnath: A significant pilgrimage destination with the Kedarnath Temple, one of the Char Dham shrines.

(9)Valley of Flowers National Park: A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its diverse flora and breathtaking landscapes.

(10)Pithoragarh: Offers historical sites like Pithoragarh Fort, Thal Kedar Temple, and the scenic Patal Bhuvaneshwar cave.
